## Summary:
I conducted a cross-cultural study to investigate alcohol consumption among first-semester university students in Germany and Colombia. Drinking behaviors, associated risk factors, and social influences were compared using online surveys completed by 171 German and 189 Colombian students. Data analysis with SPSS included t-tests, chi-square tests, Mann-Whitney tests, and regression analyses.
